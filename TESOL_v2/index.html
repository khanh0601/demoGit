Since 2006, VUS has hosted annual TESOL conferences and webinars with free registration for everyone in the ELT community in Vietnam and neighboring countries in order to cater to ELT teachersâ€™ need to improve themselves professionally. The events have created exciting opportunities for researchers, teachers, and school administrators to share ideas and practices in their profession, and keep their minds refreshed with up-to-date ELT trends. Featuring highly informative, enlightening lectures by renowned ELT authorities, educators, and book authors such as David Nunan, Alan Maley, Gordon Lewis, Allen Davenport, Ha Van Sinh, Edmund Dudley, and Derek Spafford, among others, annual VUS TESOL conferences have become one of the most eagerly-awaited professional rendezvous for thousands of teachers both inside and outside Vietnam.

With the aim of becoming the leading education system in Vietnam, VUS wishes to contribute to the education sector by equipping young generations with global values and preparing them to be even more connected and integrated with the world.

Via educational partnerships with the world's leading publishers such as National Geographic Learning, Oxford University Press, Cambridge University Press and Assessment, Macmillan, among others, VUS has offered learners internationally standardized programs that help them unlock a bright future.
